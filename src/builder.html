Quiet Book Personalizzati

Create your own book for your child. You can customize each page, create a cover with your name and choose the number of pages.

1

Età del bimbo*

Depending on the child's age, we can shape the complexity of the mechanisms, the richness of the page, and the overall content of the book.

2

Numero di pagine

Choose how many pages you want to have in the book. Minimum 4 pages excluding the title page. Keep in mind that the final price and sometimes the production time depends on the number of pages.

3

Сopertina personalizzata

Choose a cover type. You can make the book personalized for your child.

4

Сopertina semplice

Choose a design for the outer side binding of the pages. This will give your book a personalized look.

5

Сopertina semplice

Collect the page types you want to see in your book. Attention: The order of the pages depends on the order you added in the designer.
